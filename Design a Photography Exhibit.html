• The Colosseum, Rome

    Explore the grand amphitheater of the Roman Empire, an iconic symbol of ancient architectural genius and gladiatorial history.

  • Eiffel Tower, Paris

    Witness the breathtaking views of the Paris skyline from the monumental iron lattice tower located on the Champ de Mars.

  • Taj Mahal, Agra

    Admire the stunning white marble mausoleum, universally admired as a masterpiece of Mughal architecture and enduring love.

  • Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kyoto

    Walk through thousands of vibrant vermilion torii gates winding up Mount Inari in this serene Japanese Shinto shrine.
